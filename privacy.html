Privacy Policy

The Spark by What Comes Next? LLC

Effective date: April 28, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 28, 2026

The short version

Your health data is processed on hardware What Comes Next controls. It does not go to OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, or any other third-party AI provider.

We do not sell your data. We do not have an advertising model. We do not share your information with data brokers.

This is not a typical privacy policy. We mean it.

Who we are

What Comes Next? LLC is a technology company based in Ardmore, Pennsylvania. We build AI-powered personal development tools that run on infrastructure we control.

Contact: coach@whatcomesnextllc.ai
Website: whatcomesnextllc.ai

What we collect

When you use The Spark, you provide:

  • Your name, age, and occupation
  • A description of your personal wellness goal
  • A description of your daily routine
  • Daily habit check-ins and journal entries during your 7-day Evolution cycle

We also collect:

  • A push notification token to deliver reminders to your device (iOS via Apple APNs, Android via Google FCM)
  • Basic usage data sufficient to operate the service

We do not collect location data, biometric data, financial information, or any data beyond what you explicitly provide.

How your data is processed

This is the part that is different.

When you submit a wellness intake through The Spark, your data travels over an encrypted connection to an API endpoint operated by What Comes Next. It is then processed by a locally-hosted AI model running on hardware What Comes Next physically controls. At no point does your intake data touch OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, or any other third-party AI provider's infrastructure.

Your Evolution data — your check-ins, journal entries, and habit progress — is stored as plain text files on What Comes Next-controlled servers. It is not stored in a third-party cloud database. It is not processed by external AI services.

We use Resend to deliver your wellness snapshot by email. Your email address and snapshot content are transmitted to Resend for this purpose only. Resend's privacy policy is available at resend.com/legal/privacy-policy.

How we use your data

We use your data to:

  • Generate your personalized wellness snapshot
  • Store your Evolution progress so you can access it across sessions
  • Send you habit reminders and check-in prompts via push notification
  • Deliver your wellness snapshot by email if you request it

We do not use your data to train AI models. We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers.

Data retention

Your Evolution data is retained on our servers for as long as you use the service. We do not currently offer automatic deletion on a fixed schedule, but you can request deletion at any time (see below).

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what data we hold about you
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at coach@whatcomesnextllc.ai with the subject line "Data Request". We will respond within 30 days.

Data deletion requests

To request deletion of your account and all associated data:

Email coach@whatcomesnextllc.ai with the subject line "Delete My Data" and include the name you used when creating your account. We will confirm deletion within 30 days.

This is the data deletion link provided to Google Play and the Apple App Store.

Push notifications

We use push notifications to send habit reminders and check-in prompts. You can disable push notifications at any time through your device's notification settings. Disabling notifications does not delete your account or data.

Children's privacy

The Spark is intended for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

Changes to this policy

We will update this page if our practices change and will note the updated date at the top. We will not reduce your rights under this policy without explicit notice.
